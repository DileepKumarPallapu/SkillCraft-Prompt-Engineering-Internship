# Task 2 - Prompting for Creativity

## Objective

Explore how prompt structure and role assignment influence creative AI outputs.

### Prompt

Act as a startup mentor.

Generate 5 innovative AI startup ideas for Indian college students.

For each startup idea provide:

1. Startup Name
2. Problem Being Solved
3. Target Users
4. Revenue Model
5. Estimated Budget (Under ₹50,000)

### AI Generated Startup Ideas

#### 1. SmartNotes AI

Problem: Students spend hours preparing notes.
Target Users: College students.
Revenue Model: Subscription.
Budget: ₹15,000.

#### 2. InterviewAce AI

Problem: Lack of interview preparation.
Target Users: Freshers and final-year students.
Revenue Model: Freemium.
Budget: ₹20,000.

#### 3. Local Language Tutor AI

Problem: Language barriers in education.
Target Users: Rural students.
Revenue Model: Subscription and advertisements.
Budget: ₹30,000.

#### 4. AI Resume Builder

Problem: Poor resume quality.
Target Users: Students and job seekers.
Revenue Model: Premium templates.
Budget: ₹10,000.

#### 5. CampusConnect AI

Problem: Students miss internship opportunities.
Target Users: College students.
Revenue Model: Recruiter subscriptions.
Budget: ₹25,000.

## Reflection

Role-based prompting helped generate more creative and practical ideas. Providing a specific role and structured requirements improved the relevance and quality of the responses.

## Conclusion

Creative prompting can be used to generate innovative ideas, business concepts, and content with greater detail and originality.
